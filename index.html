What is it?

"The Chronicles of Virs" is a brand new homebrew setting for your Dungeons and Dragons adventures. Scale the windy mountains, trudge through enchanted swamps, visit flying cities, and meet many new and unique creatures and peoples.

Why Play?

Virs is an expansive world that combines many aspects of favorite realms in the D&D metaverse, such as Faerun and Eberron, with touches of inspiration from Magic: The Gathering's Innistrad and Ravnica. This amalagam of tone and flavor invites players to fully enjoy themselves in both roleplay and combat, the limits truly being left to your dungeon master and you!

But Beware

Not all who wander this world are as they seem, keep an eye on the inky shadows that may surround you when least expected.
